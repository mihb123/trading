Price Action Trading Strategy - Key Points from TradingStrategyGuides.com

Price action is simply how the price will react at certain levels of resistance or support.

Top 5 Price Action Trading Secrets:
1. Location is more important than the price action itself
2. Price action follows the path of least resistance (the trend)
3. Price action follows three phases: accumulation, markup, distribution
4. Price action moves from small to big price ranges
5. Price action is the footprint of institutional money

Four Steps to Master Price Action:
Step 1: Analyze Swing Highs and Swing Lows
Step 2: Measure Distance Between Swing Points
Step 3: Real Support/Resistance with Big Candle Wicks
Step 4: Combine Price Action with Indicators

Pin Bar Strategy: Find pin bar (wick 2/3 of total), check context, enter at close, SL beyond wick, TP at S/R levels.

Top Books: Al Brooks Trading Price Action, Nison Japanese Candlestick, Bulkowski Chart Patterns, Connors Street Smarts.
